Red Orange is a global full-service government contracting company. We have international offices held in Australia, Canada, India, Middle East and the United Kingdom. Our headquarters is based at 500 Office Center Drive Ste 400, Fort Washington, PA 19034. Serving the North and South American markets. A majority of our work is done through partnering with commercial companies to provide services and goods to the U.S. Federal government.

Red Orange is an in-house solutions firm for government contracting.Our core competencies include providing goods and services to the Federal Government at the most cost-effective prices and providing unmatched customer service to ensure each request is fulfilled as expected. With that being said, our procurement team handles each contract through the full bidding cycle.

Job Description

We are looking for a committed, proactive Contracts Coordinator Assistant to work collaboratively with our Sales team on specific short- and long-term projects. In this role, you will oversee tasks delegated to you by the Manager, coordinate with all necessary vendors and service providers, and ensure that the project is moving forward on time and on budget. You will also assist in client relations by being the main point of contact for questions and issues. The Contracts Coordinator Assistant needs to be highly organized, adaptable, and able to prioritize tasks while working independently.

Job Location

500 Office Center Drive, Suite 400, Fort Washington, PA 19034

In Person or Remote

In Person

Preferences

Bachelors in Business Administration, 2-3 years experience

Salary

$40k - $50k, plus benefits and incentives

Responsibilities

  • Serving as the main point of contact for clients and vendors, working to ensure that contract terms are met throughout the duration of the contract

  • Reviewing and tracking service contracts and renewals

  • Ensuring consistent management of documents, and that they are properly entered in organizational databases and securely maintained

  • Resolving client questions, issues, and complaints

  • Communicating between clients and internal teams

  • Keeping up with profitability statements for projects

Requirements

  • Able to multitask, prioritize, and manage time efficiently

  • Self-motivated and self-directed

  • Bachelor’s degree in marketing, business administration, sales, or relevant field

  • Basic computer skills, and experience with CRM software and the Microsoft Office Suite

In Person

Who We Are

RedOrange is a globally networked full-service government contractor. We are an all- inclusive, in-house solutions firm that works predominately with federal government agencies in the United States, United Kingdom and European Union. Our core competency is providing tailor-made solutions according to our clients’ needs. As a flat structured organization, we empower our teams to focus on serving our customers, making us very agile.

We have international oﬃces in Australia, Canada, India, the Middle East, and United Kingdom, and serves North and South American markets. We are headquartered at 500 Office Center Drive, Suite 400, Fort Washington, PA 19034. A majority of our work is done by partnering with commercial companies to provide products and services to the U.S. Federal Government.

We deliver goods and services at the most cost-eﬀective price while providing unmatched customer service to ensure each request is fulfilled as expected. Our procurement team handles each contract through the full bidding cycle.
